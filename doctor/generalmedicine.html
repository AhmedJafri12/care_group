About General Medicine

General Medicine, also known as Internal Medicine, is the branch of medicine that deals with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of adult diseases. Physicians trained in General Medicine are often the first point of contact for patients and play a vital role in managing both acute and chronic conditions.

Our General Medicine Department provides comprehensive care ranging from routine check-ups, vaccinations, and health screenings to the management of complex illnesses such as diabetes, hypertension, infectious diseases, and respiratory disorders. Our doctors emphasize a holistic approach that addresses both physical and lifestyle factors affecting health.

At HealthCare Hospital, we believe in patient-centered care. Our internists collaborate with specialists across departments to ensure accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment plans. We also focus on preventive healthcare, encouraging early detection and healthy lifestyle practices to improve long-term well-being.
